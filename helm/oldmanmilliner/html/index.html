History of Hats

Hats are worn for various reasons, either for fashion or protection or other occassions. There are many different types of hats around the world which are worn by almost everybody. Men, women, teachers, etc. Basically hats are a symbol of authority and power since it contains your thoughts and actions. Here are some tips and tricks about the different styles of hats.

Fedora

Tips on How to wear your Fedora

  • Make sure your fedora fits you perfectly.

  • Choose a fedora that matches your style

  • Wear it on special occassions

  • Dress your fedora down

  • Wear it with jeans
